Picture-a-day

Rediscovering my inner photographer

Something old and something new.

Photography has fallen to the wayside in the last few years. Instead of letting my camera continue to collect dust for the next few years, I've been challenged to take one picture a day for 30 days. I'm also using this as an opportunity to learn some website basics and web design. (Thanks to Steven and Dani for inspiration!) So this is all one big experiment. Enjoy!
